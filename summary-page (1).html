Summary

My professional persona is built upon core values of empathy, honesty, and responsibility. Throughout my academic and professional journey, I have consistently sought feedback and engaged in self-reflection to identify areas of strength and opportunities for growth.

In my leadership roles within clubs and organizations, I have applied these values by fostering inclusive environments where all members feel valued and motivated to contribute. Survey results from peers and mentors have consistently highlighted my ability to balance assertiveness with collaboration, creating paths forward while maintaining strong interpersonal connections.

People Styles

My assessment results identify me as primarily a "Driver" style, characterized by high assertiveness combined with moderate responsiveness. This style manifests in my approach to academic projects and professional responsibilities, where I often take initiative in establishing clear objectives and timelines while remaining attentive to team dynamics.

As a tour guide for prospective students, my Driver style enables me to confidently lead groups while adapting my presentation to audience needs. This balance of direction and flexibility has proven effective in various leadership contexts, from classroom project management to club event organization.
